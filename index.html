About

Welcome to Politico Today! My name is Jamela, a blog writer with a passion for all things politics. I started Politico Today with the idea of helping political enthusiasts like yourself, by offering your very own blog site focusing solely on the analysis of the G7 and other international superpowers.

If you are like me then you have trouble understanding and keeping up with the political world, don't worry I've got you covered! I promise to do my best to keep you updated on current events, get a better understanding of political matters - because I know it’s a messy venture with difficult jargon-, and analyze the G7 and other superpowers. After spending years being a compliance analyst on the G7, focusing solely on the compliancy of the superpowers, I became inspired to begin looking at the G7 with a more analytical lens. Because let’s be honest, the G7 isn’t perfect. After doing some research, I discovered there is no blog focusing on the G7. This inspired me to use my extensive knowledge and simplify global affairs for all my readers. If you need anything don't hesitate to contact me!
